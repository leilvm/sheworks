About She Works Rwanda

Our Mission

She Works Rwanda is dedicated to empowering women in Rwanda by providing skills, resources, and opportunities for economic independence. Our mission is to create an inclusive space where women can thrive professionally and personally.

Our History

Founded with a vision to bridge the gender gap in the workforce, She Works Rwanda has been instrumental in equipping women with digital and entrepreneurial skills. Over the years, we have impacted numerous lives through training programs, mentorship, and networking opportunities.

Meet the Team

Our team comprises dedicated professionals, mentors, and volunteers who are passionate about fostering women's empowerment. With diverse backgrounds and expertise, we work collaboratively to drive positive change in our community.
